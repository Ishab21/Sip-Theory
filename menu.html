Our Menu

Campfire Dreams

A mysterious and sophisticated drink with charcoal-grey smoky lapsang souchong tea, black tapioca pearls, and creamy foam. Perfect for those who love bold, deep flavors.

Jade Mist

A fresh and vibrant boba drink in a clear glass. Bottom layer with emerald green matcha-flavored popping boba pearls, middle layer is a beautiful jade green milk tea infused with smoky hojicha and fresh matcha creating an earthy-sweet profile, topped with white cream foam garnished with a sprinkle of matcha powder. Delicate smoke wisps rise from the top.

Smoky Sakura

An elegant fusion drink with cherry wood-smoked sakura milk tea, crystal boba, and delicate floral garnish. Sophisticated yet playful.

Dragon's Breath

A bold and adventurous drink with smoky chili-chocolate fusion, creating a spicy-sweet profile with dramatic presentation. For the daring customer! Each drink maintains that beautiful layered aesthetic with the signature smoke effect and creative flavor profiles.

Midnight Smoke

A mysterious and sophisticated drink with charcoal-grey smoky lapsang souchong tea, black tapioca pearls, and creamy foam. Perfect for those who love bold, deep flavors.

Sip Theory

Sip Theory is a concept boba brand centered around ritual, calm, and sensory storytelling. Every drink is crafted to slow you down and savor the moment.
