About Us

At Sip Theory, every drink is crafted as a ritual of calm—an invitation to slow down, breathe deep, and savor the moment. Inspired by the serenity of a spa and the warmth of a cozy hideaway, our boba is not just made, but thoughtfully composed. Each cup is layered with intention: gentle smoke that drifts like incense, delicate textures that unfold sip by sip, and flavors that reveal their depth gradually, never all at once.

We believe a drink should feel like an experience. From the first glance to the last sip, our blends are designed to awaken the senses—soft foam meeting bold tea, subtle sweetness balanced with smoky undertones, and pearls resting like hidden treasures at the bottom of the glass. Every layer tells a story, encouraging you to discover something new beneath the surface. Rooted in craft, comfort, and quiet luxury, Sip Theory transforms boba into an art form—where relaxation meets curiosity, and every sip feels unforgettable.
